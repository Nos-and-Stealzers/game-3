Arena Drift

You drift through a sealed arena while drones close in. Collect energy cells to score points, then unlock a faster survival challenge. The enemy pattern is readable, so the game stays tough without being cheap.

Controls: WASD or arrow keys. Objective: collect 10 cells and survive as long as possible.

Run Over

Save: best score, cells cleared, and unlocked difficulty.
