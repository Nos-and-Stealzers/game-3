Shipyard Scramble

A small survival shooter with a strong objective loop: collect fuel pods to keep the ship moving while hostiles patrol the dock. The pace ramps fairly, and the save keeps your longest run and upgrade level.

Controls: WASD to move, hold space to fire. Objective: keep fuel above zero and survive as long as you can.

Dock Breached
