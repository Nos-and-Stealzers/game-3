Cave Hopper

A compact platformer with readable jumps and simple hazards. Collect enough crystals, then reach the exit door to finish the run. The save keeps your best time and unlocked stage.

Controls: A/D or arrows to move, W/Up/Space to jump. Objective: collect 6 crystals and reach the door.

You Made It

Local save stores best time, best coin count, and unlocked stage.
